History

Three Decades of Music & Teaching

Based in Ottawa, Canada, Dan Tremblay has become known as much for his blend of ambient and electronic music as for his music instruction. Having gotten an early start under the careful guidance of his mother, he has since shared his several decades of musical knowledge with hundreds of students. On 8 occasions, his students have received provincial awards for the highest mark in their respective grade in piano performance. His teaching has since expanded to online course offerings covering topics including piano improvisation and cinematic music production.

Pivoting to online performance on Twitch in 2021 led to numerous production and mixing opportunities for international artists. This time also sparked a flurry of cinematic album releases under his own name, as well as several collaborations, including a song to be featured in an upcoming VR game.

His instrumental compositions ranging from delicate and sparse piano, to epic and cinematic have been licensed worldwide.

Recently, his YouTube channel has garnered attention from both composers and audio developers alike. This resulted in production work for New Zealand born composer, Christopher Norton, as well as content creation and commissioned work for Emergence Audio. He regularly reviews products for Fracture Sounds, Soundiron, Westwood Instruments, Sonixinema, Frozen Plain, Slate & Ash, Field Tone Audio and Filine Technologies.
