About Dan

Dan Tremblay is a piano instructor and producer with over three decades of experience. His dedication to teaching has led his students to success at the provincial level, while his own compositions have been licensed worldwide for multi-media productions.

Dan's popular YouTube channel has connected him with leading music technology companies and internationally recognized composers such as Christopher Norton. Alongside teaching, he continues to produce music for artists and game soundtracks, keeping his work current and inspiring.
